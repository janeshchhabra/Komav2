About Us

Komakase is led by a dynamic duo, Josh and Kieran.

Josh is a seasoned home coffee enthusiast, bringing years of experience and a deep appreciation for the art of coffee brewing.

Kieran is a seasoned coffee professional, who offers a wealth of knowledge and expertise in the industry.

Together, we're passionate about sharing our love for coffee and showcasing it's incredible range of flavors. We aim to create a unique coffee omakase experience for coffee lovers and the curious alike.
